Welcome to my website!

I am a postdoctoral fellow in Dr. [Nilanjan Chatterjee](https://nilanjanchatterjee.org/)'s group at the [Department of Biostatistics](https://www.jhsph.edu/departments/biostatistics/), [Johns Hopkins Bloomberg School of Public Health](https://www.jhsph.edu/). I received my Ph.D. in Biostatistics from the University of Minnesota in 2019. My advisors are Drs. [Joeseph S. Koopmeiners](https://directory.sph.umn.edu/bio/sph-a-z/joseph-koopmeiners) and [Lin Zhang](https://directory.sph.umn.edu/bio/sph-a-z/lin-zhang). I received my Bachelor's degree in statistics from the [University of Science and Technology of China (USTC)](https://en.ustc.edu.cn/) under the supervision of Dr. [Laisheng Wei](https://bs.ustc.edu.cn/english/profile-202.html).

 My research interests include Bayesian hierarchical models for high-dimensional, complex-structured data and statistical methods for integrating multi-source data to address cutting-edge public health issues.
